1. Introduction

Food Rush Tycoon is a mobile game developed by Null Game Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user and device data in compliance with Google Play’s User Data Policy and Families Policy Requirements. Food Rush Tycoon is suitable for all age groups, including children under the age of 13.

2. Information We Collect

Food Rush Tycoon does not collect any personal data from users. We do not request, store, or process:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Account information
  • Precise location data
  • Sensitive personal information

Non-personal Data via Third-Party Services

Some anonymous device information may be collected automatically by trusted third parties.

a) Google Firebase Analytics

Firebase may collect:

  • Device model and OS
  • Approximate country/region
  • Gameplay and usage statistics
  • Crash reports

b) Google AdMob

AdMob may collect:

  • Anonymous device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • Basic app interaction data
  • Non-personal ad engagement data

3. How We Use Data

Data processed by third-party services is used only for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Improving game performance
  • Fixing bugs and crashes

We do not collect personal data, track users across apps, sell data, or perform profiling.

4. Children’s Privacy (Under 13)

Food Rush Tycoon is suitable for children under 13. We comply with Google Play Families Policy:

  • No personal data is collected from children.
  • AdMob is configured to show family-appropriate ads.
  • No personalized or behaviorally targeted ads are shown to children.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob – advertisements
  • Google Firebase Analytics – anonymous analytics and crash reports

6. Internet Usage

The app uses the internet only for loading ads and sending anonymous analytics or crash data. No other communication is made.

7. Data Security

While we do not collect personal information, we ensure that third-party services use secure, industry-standard data protection practices.
